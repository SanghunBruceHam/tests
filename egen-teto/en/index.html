Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How accurate is the Estrogen vs Testosterone test?
A. This test is designed for entertainment and self-reflection based on psychological concepts. While it provides insights into personality traits, it should be viewed as a fun exploration tool rather than a clinical assessment.
Q. What do Estrogen and Testosterone types mean?
A. These are personality types inspired by hormone characteristics - Estrogen represents emotional, empathetic traits while Testosterone represents logical, analytical traits. They don't reflect actual hormone levels but personality tendencies.
Q. Can my results change over time?
A. Yes, personality and circumstances can evolve over time, so your results may vary in different periods. Taking the test periodically might reveal interesting changes in your personality.
